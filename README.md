# TIGGO

TIGGO is an AI-powered platform designed to support space discovery, project planning, collaboration, and community engagement.

## Modules

### 1. Service Provider (SP)

The Service Provider module collects and analyzes data from government portals and private sources using AI, presenting valuable insights to users.

#### Features

##### User Dashboard
- View user activities
- Manage projects
- Track associated teams

##### Pulse
- Explore articles and insights related to spaces
- Stay updated with trends and developments

##### Project
- Discover project opportunities
- Explore projects available for modification and enhancement

##### Collaborate
- Invite individuals to teams
- Request to join teams based on user roles and interests

#### Team Roles

- **Trailblazer** – Team leader responsible for driving project success
- **Community Whisper** – Connects communities with project teams and gathers insights
- **Beacon** – Provides strategic ideas and recommendations
- **Master Mind** – Solves complex challenges and critical issues
- **Gate Keeper** – Ensures compliance, governance, and project integrity

---

### 2. Project Provider

The Project Provider module helps users create and evaluate development proposals through a structured workflow.

#### Workflow

##### Step 1: Select and Envision
- Select a location from the map
- Define the vision for the site

##### Step 2: Explore Connections
- Analyze context and surroundings
- Evaluate proximity and demographic insights

##### Step 3: Evaluate the Site
- Assess project readiness
- Review environmental and community factors such as:
  - Air quality
  - Community support
  - Soil conditions

##### Step 4: Discover the Best Fit
- Identify suitable development opportunities such as:
  - Parks
  - Wellness centers
  - Office spaces
  - Community facilities

##### Step 5: Engage Stakeholders
- Connect with communities and primary users
- Gather feedback through:
  - Surveys
  - Posters
  - Community outreach programs

##### Step 6: Plan the Budget
- Estimate project costs
- Explore funding opportunities
- Review revenue potential
- Select budget strategies:
  - Standard
  - Lean
  - Premium

## Technology Stack

- Frontend: React.js
- Backend: FastAPI
- Database: PostgreSQL
- AI & Data Processing
- REST APIs

## Goal

TIGGO aims to simplify project discovery, planning, collaboration, and decision-making through AI-driven insights and community engagement.
